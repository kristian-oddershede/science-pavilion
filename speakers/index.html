Information for Speakers

Alt du behøver at vide om din deltagelse ved Science Pavilion på Smukfest 2026

Everything you need to know about your participation at the Science Pavilion, Smukfest 2026

Ankomst og transport

Arrival and Transport

Når du ankommer til Smukfest, bedes du henvende dig ved indgangen Sherwood. Her kan du afhente dit adgangsarmbånd, hvis du ikke allerede har modtaget det via email. Vi anbefaler at tjekke spam-mappen, hvis du ikke har modtaget det.

Upon arrival at Smukfest, please head to the Sherwood entrance. This is where you collect your access wristband if you haven't already received it via email. We recommend checking your spam folder if you haven't received it.

  • Har du problemer med adgang: email gaesteliste@smukfest.dk eller kontakt Jørgen (+45 35 32 54 43)
  • Access issues: email gaesteliste@smukfest.dk or contact Jørgen (+45 35 32 54 43)
  • Ankom gerne 30 minutter før dit oplæg og tjek ind hos Lars/Jørgen.
  • Please arrive 30 minutes before your presentation and check in with Lars/Jørgen.
  • Der er shuttlebusser fra togstationen til festivalområdet “Kærligheden” og videre til hovedindgangen.
  • Shuttle buses run from the train station to the "Kærligheden" area and onwards to the main entrance.
  • Ved ankomst i bil anbefales det at starte ved festivalens kontor: Birkevej 23, Skanderborg, hvor korttidsparkering er mulig.
  • When arriving by car, it is recommended to start at the festival office: Birkevej 23, Skanderborg, where short-term parking is available.

Parkering

Parking

Oplægsholdere kan parkere ved områderne Elefanten eller Giraffen uden parkeringskort. Shuttlebus kører derfra til hovedindgangen.

Speakers can park at the Elefanten or Giraffen areas without a parking permit. A shuttle bus runs from there to the main entrance.

  • Adresse til oplægsholderparkering: Elefanten / Giraffen (følg festivalens skilte)
  • Address for speaker parking: Elefanten / Giraffen (follow the festival signs)
  • Kom i god tid, da områderne kan være travle
  • Arrive in good time as these areas can get busy
  • Shuttlebus kører hele dagen mellem parkering og hovedindgang
  • The shuttle bus runs all day between the parking areas and the main entrance

Accommodation and Transport from Hotel

Hotels are booked for speakers (Scandic Opus Horsens). A limo service runs between the hotel and the festival site from 05.08.2025 at 08:00 until 09.08.2025 at 02:00.

  • The trip takes approx. 15–30 minutes depending on traffic – please book in advance.

Teknisk udstyr

Technical Equipment

Science Pavilion stiller til rådighed:

Science Pavilion provides:

  • Projektor og lærred
  • Projector and screen
  • Lydanlæg med mikrofoner (håndholdt og headset)
  • Sound system with microphones (handheld and headset)
  • HDMI-adgang til præsentationer
  • HDMI access for presentations
  • Teknisk assistance under opsætning
  • Technical assistance during setup

Medbring gerne dit oplæg på USB eller send det på forhånd, hvis du bruger egen laptop. PDF anbefales for kompatibilitet.

Please bring your presentation on a USB drive or send it in advance if you are using your own laptop. PDF is recommended for compatibility.

Lokation

Location

Science Pavilion ligger på festivalpladsen midt i skoven ved Skanderborg Sø. Det anbefales som nævnt at du først besøger korttidsparkering ved hovedkontoret hvor du også samler dit armbånd op. Se kortet herunder:

The Science Pavilion is located on the festival grounds in the middle of the forest by Skanderborg Lake. As mentioned, it is recommended to first visit the short-term parking at the main office where you also collect your wristband. See the map below:

Vejr og påklædning

Weather and Clothing

Sommervejret forventes typisk dansk med sol og enkelte byger, omkring 20°C. Da festivalen ligger midt i skoven, anbefales robust og komfortabelt fodtøj.

The summer weather is expected to be typically Danish with sun and occasional showers, around 20°C. As the festival is located in the forest, sturdy and comfortable footwear is recommended.

Accreditering og adgang

Accreditation and Access

Adgangsarmbånd udleveres ved Sherwood-indgangen. Hvert oplægsholderarmbånd har 300 DKK til forbrug på festivalens terminaler. Husk at informere kassereren inden betaling.

Access wristbands are issued at the Sherwood entrance. Each speaker wristband includes 300 DKK for use at the festival terminals. Remember to inform the cashier before paying.
